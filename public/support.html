Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password or need to reset it, go to the login screen of the chalofy app and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive instructions to create a new password. Follow the prompts to reset your password and regain access to your account.

2. How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile details, open the app and navigate to the "Profile" section found in the app settings. Here, you can edit your personal information, such as your name, contact details, and profile picture. Once you've made the necessary changes, ensure you save them to update your profile.

3. How do I report a bug or issue with the app?

If you encounter a bug or any issues while using the app, please report it to us by emailing chalofyrentals@gmail.com. Provide a detailed description of the issue, including any error messages or screenshots if available. This information helps us resolve the problem more efficiently and improve the app's performance.

4. How do I cancel my property booking?

To cancel a booking, open the app and go to the "Bookings" section. Select the reservation you wish to cancel and follow the provided instructions. Depending on the property's terms, you may receive a confirmation of the cancellation and information about any applicable fees or refunds.

5. How do I provide feedback or suggest improvements?

We welcome your feedback and suggestions to help us enhance the chalofy experience. You can send your comments and ideas to chalofyrentals@gmail.com. Your input is valuable in helping us make continuous improvements to our app.
